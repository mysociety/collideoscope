How Collideoscope works

  • Find a location by postcode, street, or landmark
  • Tell us a few details about your collision or near-miss
  • We notify the council’s highways department
  • And make statistics available to planners and campaigners

Need cycling collision data?

Collideoscope is building into a massive nationwide archive of data on cycling collisions and near-misses, which you can use for free.

Browse and download our data for campaigners, cyclists, and road safety experts

As well as our users’ reports, our statistics and maps also include police STATS19 data, for a comprehensive visualisation of cycling incidents.
