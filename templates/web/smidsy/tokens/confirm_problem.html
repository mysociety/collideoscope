Why report it?

Drivers who cause injury or damage must either stop and exchange details with the other parties involved, or report the incident to the police. If they don’t, they’re in breach of Section 170 of the Road Traffic Act 1988.

The police will often also want to know more about the incident—including video or photo evidence, if you have it—to determine whether the driver could be charged with a dangerous driving offence.
